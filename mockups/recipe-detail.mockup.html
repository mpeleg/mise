Pasta al Limone

30 min
2 servings
Italian Vegetarian Quick
nytcooking.com

Ingredients

Spaghetti 200g
Lemon 1, zested + juiced
Parmesan 60g, finely grated
Butter 2 tbsp
Garlic 1 clove
Black pepper to taste
Salt to taste

Notes

Add more lemon zest next time. A pinch of chilli flakes works really well too.

Pasta al Limone

Steps

1

Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti until al dente, reserving 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

2

While the pasta cooks, zest the lemon and finely grate the parmesan. Mince the garlic clove.

3

Melt butter in a wide pan over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant but not browned.

4

Add the drained pasta to the pan. Pour in the lemon juice and a splash of pasta water. Toss to combine.

5

Remove from heat. Add parmesan and lemon zest, tossing vigorously until the sauce is creamy. Add more pasta water as needed.

6

Season generously with black pepper and serve immediately.
